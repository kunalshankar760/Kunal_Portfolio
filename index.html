About Me

I am a responsible and dedicated self-starter who thrives in both independent and collaborative environments. With a strong work ethic and a drive to achieve the best outcomes, I take initiative, communicate effectively, and adapt quickly to new challenges.

My current research focuses on optimizing parameters for thermal building models using grey-box techniques. I aim to develop scalable representations of HVAC dynamics that can be integrated into day-ahead operational planning for TSOs/DSOs. This involves estimating thermal and behavioral parameters, formulating tractable optimization models, and supporting system-level goals such as cost-efficient energy use through aggregated building flexibility.
